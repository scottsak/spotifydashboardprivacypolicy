Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 31st, 2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains our policy regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Chrome extension ("the Extension").

2. Data Collection

We want to make it clear that our Extension does not collect, store, or share any personal data or information from its users. This includes any data related to your Spotify account or any other data that might be accessible through the use of the Extension.

3. Integration with Spotify

Our Extension integrates with Spotify to enhance your experience. However, we do not collect, store, or share any data from your Spotify account or your usage of Spotify services. All interactions with Spotify occur directly between your browser and Spotify's servers, with no data being intercepted or stored by our Extension.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
